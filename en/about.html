About Clinical Pilates

A personalized therapeutic approach for your well-being

What is Clinical Pilates?

Clinical Pilates is a specialized form of therapeutic exercise that combines the principles of traditional Pilates with knowledge of physiotherapy and biomechanics.

Specifically developed to prevent and treat injuries, Clinical Pilates is adapted to each person's individual needs, respecting their specific limitations and goals.

Each session is carefully planned to improve strength, flexibility, posture, and motor control, promoting conscious and functional movement.

Professional Equipment

Our studio is equipped with high-quality Reformer equipment, specially designed for Clinical Pilates practice.

Each piece of equipment allows precise resistance and position adjustments, ensuring that exercises are perfectly adapted to your individual needs and capabilities.

We provide a safe, comfortable, and professional environment for your Clinical Pilates practice.
